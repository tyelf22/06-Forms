Who should attend

You should attend this race if you are wanting to get a new PR for your marathon run.
Much of the race is downhill which will help you get a better time. If you are an experienced
runner or if you just want to try something new this is a great opportunity.

You should attend if you are...

  • Runners
  • Fitness Enthusiasts
  • Body Builders
  • People with active lifestyles
  • Anyone wanting to get fit

What you will accomplish

  1. Getting fit
  2. Be Mentally stronger
  3. Feeling of accomplishment
  4. Burn calories
  5. Build muscle
