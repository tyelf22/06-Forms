About the Marathon

The Marathon is going to be taken place at Big Cotton Wood Canyon in Salt Lake City, Utah.
We will meet at the top of the canyon at 5:30 a.m. and the race will then begin at 6:30 a.m.
Staff members will be there to provide everyone with nutritional snacks and sport drinks. Bathrooms
will be available at the starting line and every 2 miles of course.

Aid Station
Also called a water stop. Any point along the course that offers water and sports drinks,
handed out by volunteers. Often, at bigger races, people also hand out gels, energy bars, and other items. Here is a link to proper aid station etiquette.
Bib
The sheets printed with numbers (called “bib numbers”) used to identify each runner in a race.
Personal Record (PR)
Term used to describe a runner’s farthest or fastest time in a race. Also called a Personal Best (PB). Here is a link on how to improve your PR.
